Why this kolaveri di webpage?

During 2021-22, I lost my mind while trying to find out the merit lists. All I could do was to take screenshots of YouTube videos and make my phone a mess. So, I thought that it would be nice to have a centralised database of all the merit lists.

What can you do?

ANYTHING. But fix my grammatical mistakes first. If I have missed some of the merit lists than you can e-mail me the lists or fork this repo on GitHub and create a pull request.

What more can be done?

Some details can be added regarding the neighbourhood of the colleges and the availability of rooms/PGs and other stuffs so that the new students get at least an idea about the the colleges.

This is our place, we make the rules. You can share more ideas to make this database better. Just e-mail me. You will get proper credit.

Am I getting any profit/money from this?

No.

What if you "steal" this site?
